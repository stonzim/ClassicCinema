Location and Scenery

The Harbour Hotel, is situated on the Otago Peninsula, and as such, offers a fantastic view of the beautiful Otago Harbour. We are only a short distance from Lanarch Castle and Glenfalloch Gardens, beaches and other beautiful areas of forestry. Otago Peninsula is also home to some of New Zealand's most iconic wildlife, such as the Royal Albatross and the Yellow Eyed Penguin.
